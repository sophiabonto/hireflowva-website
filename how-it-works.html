Step by Step

6 Steps from Assessment to Placed

Every placement follows the same proven pipeline — no shortcuts, no guesswork.

01
📋 Discovery

Free Needs Assessment

We spend 20 minutes learning your business: role requirements, tools, workflows, communication style, and what success looks like. This call shapes everything that comes after.

02
🔍 Sourcing

Sourcing & Posting

We post your role across top VA platforms and filter through 100–200+ applicants using your exact criteria. You don't see a single unvetted resume — ever.

03
✅ Vetting

Skills Assessment

Every candidate completes a real skills test built around the actual tasks your role requires — not generic aptitude quizzes. We score, rank, and advance only those who meet the bar.

04
🎙 Shortlist

Interview & Shortlist

We conduct screening interviews and send you a curated shortlist of 3–5 finalists. You hold one conversation per candidate — then pick your person. That's your only job.

05
🚀 Onboarding

Training Add-On

4 structured training sessions custom-built to your tools and workflows. Day one is productive, not a learning curve. Your VA arrives knowing your systems, not just a résumé.

06
🛡 Guarantee

30-Day Fit Guarantee

Not the right fit within 30 days? We re-run the entire process at no extra cost. No questions, no friction — just a seamless rematch so your business keeps moving.
